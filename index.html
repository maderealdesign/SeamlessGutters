About Us

Expert Guttering from Colne to Skipton

Based at Pine Garth Farm in Colne, Lancashire Aluminium Seamless Guttering provides top-tier roofline solutions across the region. We pride ourselves on delivering a flawless finish that protects your home from the UK weather.

Traditional plastic gutters are joined together, and over time, those seals perish and leak. Our system is different. We bring our forming machine directly to your property and create a single, continuous gutter measured exactly to your roofline.

  • Highly durable & rust-free
  • Environmentally friendly materials
  • Quick and clean installation
