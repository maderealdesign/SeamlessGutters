The Commercial Challenge

Protecting Your Assets & Inventory

Large commercial and industrial roofs capture vast volumes of water during heavy UK downpours. When standard, jointed plastic guttering systems fail under this sheer weight and pressure, the resulting leaks can cause catastrophic damage to inventory, machinery, and the building's structural integrity.

Lancashire Aluminium provides a permanent, high-capacity solution.

By removing joints entirely, we eliminate the structural weak points where leaks inevitably occur. Our heavy-duty aluminium profiles are extruded on-site to the exact, continuous lengths required for large-scale buildings, offering unparalleled peace of mind for property managers and business owners.

Technical Specifications

Built for Heavy-Duty Performance

Our commercial systems are engineered to handle the demands of large roof areas and extreme weather conditions.

High Capacity Flow

Deep-flow profiles designed to channel significantly higher volumes of water safely away from large commercial roof spans, preventing overflow during torrential rain.

Heavy-Duty Bracketing

Installed using concealed, extra-strength structural brackets that prevent sagging even when gutters are heavily loaded with water or winter snow.

Zero Maintenance Profile

High-grade aluminium requires no painting, resists rust completely, and with no joints to perish, ongoing maintenance costs for property managers are virtually eliminated.

Commercial Enquiries

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you handle high-capacity water flow for large commercial roofs?

Yes, our seamless aluminium gutters are extruded to handle significantly higher water volumes than standard domestic uPVC systems. The deep profile ensures swift evacuation of water, making them ideal for large commercial and industrial roof surface areas.

Will the installation disrupt my business operations?

We pride ourselves on our swift, on-site extrusion process which vastly reduces installation time compared to traditional methods. We work closely with site managers to coordinate installation times, ensuring minimal disruption to your daily operations, staff, and customers.

What is the maximum length of gutter you can install?

Because we extrude the aluminium continuously from a coil housed in our mobile machinery, we can technically run lengths up to 30 meters or more without a single joint. This continuous span is perfect for the long elevations of warehouses and factory units.
