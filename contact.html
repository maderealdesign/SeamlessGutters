Got Questions?

Quotation FAQs

Is the site survey genuinely free?

Yes. We do not charge for visiting your property, taking measurements, or providing you with a detailed quotation. There is absolutely no obligation to proceed once you receive your quote.

Do I need to be at home for the survey?

As long as we have full external access to all sides of your property where guttering is required, you do not necessarily need to be home. However, it is often helpful if you are available to discuss any specific issues you've noticed with your current roofline.

How soon can you install after a quote is accepted?

Lead times can vary depending on the season, but because we manufacture the guttering on-site rather than waiting for factory deliveries, we can often schedule your installation much sooner than traditional fitters.
