Our Quality Guarantee

Attention to Every Detail

As you can see from our gallery, we don't believe in cutting corners. The difference between a good job and a great job lies in the finishing touches.

  • Concealed Brackets: We use hidden, heavy-duty brackets inside the guttering. This provides immense strength while maintaining a clean, uninterrupted external profile.

  • Exact Dimensions: Our mobile extrusion machinery ensures your gutters are rolled to the millimeter, eliminating overhangs or shortfalls.

  • Immaculate Cleanup: We respect your property. Old materials are removed and the site is left as tidy as we found it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you install seamless guttering on older, historic properties?

Yes. As seen in our gallery, our Ogee profile aluminium guttering complements traditional stone and brick builds beautifully. It mimics the classic appearance of cast iron without the exorbitant cost, heavy weight, or rust issues.

Can I view an installation in person?

We have completed hundreds of installations across Colne, Skipton, Burnley, and the surrounding areas. If you would like to see our work locally, please get in touch and we can likely direct you to a street nearby where we have recently installed a seamless system.

Are all the photos in this gallery your own work?

Absolutely. We take immense pride in our craftsmanship. Every image in our portfolio represents a genuine installation completed by the Lancashire Aluminium Seamless Guttering team.
